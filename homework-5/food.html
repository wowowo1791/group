Restaurant Menu

You have been hired by a local restaurant to develop an online ordering system for customers. The restaurant sells hamburgers, cheeseburgers, fries, and onion rings. You must show all of the menu items and give customers a way to add them to the cart. Show the customers their cart and allow them to remove the last item they added.
